Identifying Coins and Their Values

In Malaysia, we have our own special coins. One coin is called the "sen." There are coins worth 5 sen, 10 sen, 20 sen, and 50 sen. The 5 sen coin is the smallest, and the 50 sen coin is the biggest. When we count our coins, we are counting how many sens we have. For example, a 10 sen coin is worth more than a 5 sen coin. So, when you collect these coins, you're actually collecting different amounts of money!

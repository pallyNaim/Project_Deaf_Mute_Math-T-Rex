Telling Time with Digital Clocks

Besides analog clocks, we also use digital clocks. Digital clocks show the time using numbers. The first two numbers are the hours, and the last two are the minutes. For example, if you see 07:45, it means it's 7:45. Practice reading and writing the time on digital clocks, and compare it with what you see on the regular clock.

Note Money
Budgeting and Saving

Money is like a special treasure in Malaysia too! Sometimes, we need to plan how to use our Ringgit wisely. You can make a budget by thinking about what you need, like school supplies or food, and what you want, like a new game. Save some of your money for the things you want, and it's like having your own Malaysian money plan! So, when you receive Ringgit, think about your needs, your wants, and how much you want to save.
